## Rating System
- **9.5-10**: Masterpiece, life-changing
- **9.0-9.4**: Exceptional, highly recommended
- **8.5-8.9**: Excellent, very enjoyable/worthwhile
- **8.0-8.4**: Good, worth reading
- **7.0-7.9**: Decent, has merits
- **Below 7**: Not listed
